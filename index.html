Avionics

54mm Rocket Electronics Bay

A fully assembled electronics bay for a 54mm high-power rocket. The sled mounts between two circular bulkheads on threaded standoffs and houses a flight computer and altimeter for deployment charge triggering and flight data logging. Features a barometric vent port and cutout for wiring access.

Structural

54mm HPR Coupler

A two-stage coupler for 54mm HPR airframes. A straight upper cylinder inserts into one body tube while the conical lower collar seats against the other, providing structural continuity across the airframe joint. Features alignment screw holes.

54mm-HPR-Coupler-1.step
Aerodynamics

54mm HPR Swept Fin

A swept fin for a 54mm high-power rocket with a triangular planform and beveled leading edge for reduced drag. The rectangular root tab slots into the airframe for secure, repeatable attachment — typical of fiberglass construction.

54mm-HPR-Fins-1.step
GPS / Tracking

BT-60 Jiobit GPS Holder

A BT-60 coupler with an integrated internal cradle designed to securely seat a Jiobit GPS tracker inside the rocket airframe. Enables real-time location tracking during and after flight for reliable rocket recovery.

BT-60-Coupler-Jiobit-Holder-1.step
Fabrication Tooling

BT-60 4x Fin Cutting Jig

A fabrication jig that slides over a BT-60 body tube to guide precise fin slot cuts at exactly 90° intervals. Four evenly spaced vertical slots ensure consistent fin alignment during airframe assembly, eliminating measurement error.
